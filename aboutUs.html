Agrigrow Tanzania Limited (ATL).

Agrigrow Tanzania Limited (ATL) is a private agribusiness company that offers agricultural solutions in areas of seeds, crop protection chemicals, fertilizers, bio-stimulants and technical services on crop production and management.

Since our founding and incorporation in 2014, we have successfully grown to a profitable agri-input business with a focus on:

  • importation and distribution of Spanish made
  • plant origin bio-stimulants
  • making us the first local independent agribusiness company that brings this important farm technology to farmers, thus contributing into efforts to grow the agriculture sector in Tanzania

With our headquarters in Morogoro town, we have branches in Iringa, Arusha and Dodoma where we avail our products to retailers and farmers. We also have a broad network of key agro-dealers in many areas including Mbeya, Njombe, Iringa, Morogoro, Dar es Salaam, Tanga, Kilimanjaro, Arusha and Manyara regions.
