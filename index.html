About Us

Agrigrow (T) LTD
Growing Agriculture

Agrigrow Tanzania Limited (ATL) is a private agribusiness company that offers agricultural solutions in areas of seeds, crop protection chemicals, fertilizers, bio-stimulants and technical services on crop production and management

Since our founding and incorporation in 2014, we have successfully grown to a profitable agri-input business with a focus on importation and distribution of Spanish made, plant origin bio-stimulants, making us the first local independent agribusiness company that brings this important farm technology to farmers, thus contributing into efforts to grow the agriculture sector in Tanzania
